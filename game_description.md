# Rogue-Hero: Master the Rhythm of Combat! ⚔️

Dive into a high-octane, **tempo-driven roguelike deck-builder** where every strike, dodge, and ability fuels your momentum! In Rogue-Hero, your performance dictates the pace of battle. Build your *Tempo* by chaining relentless combos and scoring perfect kills—but beware: the higher your tempo climbs, the more lethal the game becomes! 

Will you ride the razor's edge of maximum momentum for massive damage, or will you crash and burn beneath the pressure? 

**Why You'll Love It:**
- **Dynamic Tempo Combat:** A risk-vs-reward loop where going faster means hitting harder, but makes your enemies deadlier. Manage your 0-100 gauge to survive.
- **Strategic Deckbuilding:** Draft synergistic decks, balance your action points, and execute heavy strikes or perfect dodges.
- **Deep Roguelike Progression:** Battle through unique enemy types—like relentless Turrets and elusive Teleporters—while unlocking new decks and scaling up difficulty tiers.
- **Pure Action, Zero Friction:** Play instantly! Built with a relentlessly fast HTML5 Canvas engine, you can jump straight into the heart-pounding action directly in your browser or on your desktop.

*Find your rhythm. Build your deck. Become the Rogue-Hero.*
